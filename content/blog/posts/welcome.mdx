# Welcome to the New Plain License Platform

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our redesigned platform!

## What's New?

- **Git-Based CMS**: Our new headless CMS makes it easier for our team to update licenses and publish new guides.
- **Enhanced Comparison**: A completely revamped mapping engine allows you to compare plain language clauses with original legal text side-by-side.
- **Multi-Format Exports**: You can now download any of our licenses in Markdown, Plaintext, PDF, SPDX XML, and even embed them as HTML widgets.
- **Better Performance**: Built with Astro 5.x, the site is now faster than ever.

Stay tuned for more updates and simplified license guides!
