We are not lawyers. This is not legal advice. If you need legal advice, talk to a lawyer. You use this license at your own risk.

We are normal people who want to make licenses accessible for everyone. We hope that our plain language helps you and anyone else understand this license  (including lawyers). If you see a mistake or want to suggest a change, please [submit an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/plainlicense/plainlicense/issues) "Submit an issue on GitHub".
