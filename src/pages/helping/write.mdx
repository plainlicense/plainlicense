# Plain Language Writing Guidelines

Writing in plain language is essential for our mission. It makes legal documents accessible to everyone.

## Core Guidelines

- **Use Common Words**: Prefer simple, everyday language over complex legal terms.
- **Short Sentences**: Aim for one idea per sentence.
- **Active Voice**: Make it clear who is doing what.
- **Direct Address**: Use "you" and "we" to engage the reader.

## Resources

- [PlainLanguage.gov](https://www.plainlanguage.gov/)
- [The Center for Plain Language](https://centerforplainlanguage.org/)

By following these guidelines, you help us make licensing understandable for everyone.
