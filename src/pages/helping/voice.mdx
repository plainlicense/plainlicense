# Plain License Voice and Messaging Guidelines

We want our message to be clear and accessible. To do that, we use a friendly and approachable voice in all of our communication.

## Core Principles

- **Be Clear**: Use simple, direct language. Avoid jargon whenever possible.
- **Be Helpful**: Provide useful information and guidance.
- **Be Friendly**: Use a warm and welcoming tone.
- **Be Professional**: Maintain a high standard of quality and accuracy.

## Writing Tips

- **Use Active Voice**: Active voice is clearer and more engaging than passive voice.
- **Keep Sentences Short**: Short sentences are easier to read and understand.
- **Use "You" and "We"**: Using personal pronouns makes our writing feel more personal and approachable.
- **Avoid Jargon**: If you must use a technical term, explain it in simple language.

By following these guidelines, you can help us create a consistent and welcoming voice for Plain License.
